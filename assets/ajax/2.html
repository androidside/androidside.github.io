gocanto-cms

it is a completely CMS manager where you will be able to build your own web page for either startups or big companies. It was built in Codeigniter 2.x and it is able to serve all kind of contents, such as, services pages, blogs, contact page, gateway payments, hosting reseller page (using godaddy) and even more
